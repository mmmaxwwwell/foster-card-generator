Foster Card Generator

Create professional trading cards for foster animals in minutes

A free desktop application that helps animal rescue organizations create beautiful, printable promotional materials to help foster animals find their forever homes.

Download for Windows View on GitHub

Everything You Need to Promote Foster Animals

Professional Card Design

Generate high-quality 360 DPI cards with animal photos, details, and QR codes that print perfectly on Avery 8471 business card templates.

Animal Database

Store and organize all your foster animals in one place. Track names, breeds, ages, vaccination status, and compatibility information.

Auto-Import from Websites

Automatically scrape animal data from Wagtopia and Adoptapet adoption websites. No manual data entry required.

Advanced Print Settings

Save printer profiles, calibrate for perfect alignment, and batch print multiple cards. Works with any printer.

QR Codes

Each card includes a QR code linking directly to the animal's adoption profile, making it easy for potential adopters to learn more.

Multi-Rescue Support

Manage animals from multiple rescue organizations, each with their own branding and logos.

Screenshots

See Foster Card Generator in action

Main Dashboard

View all your foster animals in a beautiful grid layout. Each card shows the animal's photo, name, breed, age, and compatibility information at a glance.

Add Animals

Three ways to add animals: enter data manually, scrape from a URL, or bulk import multiple animals from a rescue organization's website.

Edit Animal Details

Edit all animal information including name, breed, age, size, vaccination status, house-training, and compatibility with kids, dogs, and cats.

Upload Photos

Easily upload animal photos using the native file picker. Photos are automatically processed and optimized for card printing.

Print Card Fronts

Configure print settings with a preview of the card fronts. Select your printer, paper size, orientation, and number of copies.

Print Card Backs

Print the backs of the cards with QR codes and detailed animal information. Flip your paper and print for double-sided cards.

Print Calibration

Fine-tune print alignment with the calibration system. Enter measurements from a test page to ensure cards print perfectly on Avery templates.

Example Output

The final printed output: 10 professional trading cards per sheet. Front cards feature the animal photo, name, and rescue branding. Back cards include QR codes, adoption URLs, and detailed compatibility information.

How It Works

1

Add Your Animals

Enter animal information manually, scrape from a URL, or bulk import from a rescue organization's website.

2

Generate Cards

The app automatically creates professional double-sided trading cards with photos, details, and QR codes.

3

Print & Share

Print on Avery 8471 business card templates and hand them out at adoption events, vet offices, or anywhere potential adopters might be.

Professional Quality Cards

Each card is designed to print at 360 DPI on standard business card templates, featuring:

Front of Card

  • Rescue organization logo
  • Organization website URL
  • Large animal portrait photo
  • Animal's name and age
  • Animal's breed

Back of Card

  • Adoption profile URL
  • QR code for easy scanning
  • Compatibility info (kids, dogs, cats)
  • Vaccination status
  • House-training status

Your Data Stays Local

Foster Card Generator stores all data locally on your computer. No cloud accounts, no subscriptions, no data collection. Your animals' information never leaves your machine.
